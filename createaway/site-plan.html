Purpose

Do you ever find yourself wanting to create something but not knowing how or where to start? Audience

Have you seen a craft online but are not sure where to start. Are you looking to learn new skills. Have you started a new project but are stuck? You have come to the right place. At Creat Away we want to help guide you from start to finish. We have many different tutorial videos for you to choose from. Come and enjoy learning new skills or strengthening ones you already have.

Content

Home page

Do you find yourself wanting to create something but are not sure where to start? You have come to the right place here at Create Away we are here to help give you tips, and tricks create your next project. Create Away was designed to help encourage you to try something new. From learning to paint to carpentry and so much more our guided tutorials will help you build the skills in the craft you desire. Most task can seem unfinishable when you look at all the steps, but as you take it one step at a time it is more manageable. We have structured the tutorials that way to help you go step by step and not get overwhelmed. Our hope is to answer the question of how to and guide them through the process. We also want to take away the doubt and thoughts of “I could never do that.” or “there is no way mine would look that good.” We want to build the positive mindset that anyone can be creative, and they can start the project they have been thinking about. Help them build the confidence in the skills they already have and to learn new ones along the way.

Learning to Paint

From Watercolor to Oil and Acrylic paints we have the courses for you. Painting is a great way to release stress, anxiety or express how you are feeling when you don’t have the words. Nadia Boulanger stated “Art is not an emotion. Art is a medium in which emotion is expressed.” Whatever you are feeling let that it flow through your painting. Though painting can seem daunting at first if you go into it knowing that it doesn’t have to be perfect you will find that it can be a freeing experience. The most amazing thing about paintings and art is that they have the ability to move people and create conversations. Bob Ross once said “We don’t make mistakes. We just have happy accidents.” Take what you think is a mistake and make it into something different. Let your paint, and canvas guide you to tell the story that wants to be told. Anyone can be a painter. Mix the colors your own way. Don’t be afraid to let your brushstrokes shoe, and above all make sure your art has a soul.

Art of Carpentry

Woodworking is a form of art that I don’t think gets talked about very often. But if you look the intricacies of a wooden door or figurine made of wood. It takes a lot of hard work and precision to create something out of wood. Each piece of wood has a personality about it. One might have a lot of knots in it, one may be blemish free. Another can have a bend to it. As you look at the wood and use the personality you will be able to create beautiful works of art. “True Art is Characterized by the irresistible urge in the creative artist.” There is nothing quite like the feeling of creating something beautiful. To take raw materials and transform them into something useful and appealing to the eye is an art. Every woodworker starts out as a beginner. Don’t be afraid to make mistakes, they are a part of learning and may be just want you need understand the wood or your tools better. There is a saying that “the laborer works with his hands, the craftsman with his head, but the artist works with his heart.”.
